ONCC.org

Complete website redesign and improved content structure. The new tagline, "Credibility. Contribution. Care." speaks to ONCC's mission to ensure nurses are well-prepared to provide the highest level of care to patients with cancer.

Problem

The ONCC website had not undergone a significant redesign since 2012. Over time, incremental content additions were layered onto an aging architecture without a cohesive content strategy or structural oversight. As a result:

  • Navigation pathways became fragmented and difficult to follow
  • Content hierarchy lacked clarity and consistency
  • Key certification and credentialing information was not easily discoverable
  • The visual design no longer reflected the professionalism and authority of oncology nursing

The organization needed more than a visual refresh. It required a strategic realignment of information architecture, brand expression, and user journeys to better support nurses seeking certification, renewal, and exam resources.

The core challenge was to modernize the experience while simplifying complex credentialing information — ensuring the site conveyed trust, credibility, and clarity.

Solution

Update the content architecture to make it easier for nursese to find the certifcations they need. Ensure the site meets WCAAG Accessibility standards. The updated branding features a lively and colorful palette designed with the cancer ecosystem in mind and how each color is associated with a different type of cancer. The new tagline, "Credibility. Contribution. Care." speaks to ONCC's mission to ensure nurses are well-prepared to provide the highest level of care to patients with cancer.

My Role

  • As the lead UX designer on the ONCC redesign, I drove the end-to-end experience strategy — conducting research and competitive benchmarking, leading content audits, and rebuilding the site’s information architecture from the ground up. I facilitated collaborative problem-solving sessions with stakeholders to align business goals with user needs, developed a new sitemap and scalable navigation model, and translated insights into wireframes and a modernized visual system that elevated both usability and brand credibility.
